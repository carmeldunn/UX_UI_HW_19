Find Your Anchor

Redesign Website for Gaining Retention


We saw an opportunity to help this non-profit organization feature their box to get users to spread suicide prevention awareness. We solved this by redesigning the homepage to highlight the box and how to get or receive it.

REAL Fast

Mobile App for the DMV REAL ID Process to Maximize Time


We saw an opportunity to make the DMV experience smooth for the user as many of us have pains of going in-person as it takes up your time. We solved this by creating an app where the user can cut down their time spent at the DMV after filling out forms online by checking in to get in line through the app from miles away and getting notified of when to come in person to complete their DMV task.
